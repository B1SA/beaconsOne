B1BeaconsDemo xCode app
===============

A SAP Business One iOS demo app using Ubudu SDK to get beacons information and push promotions to the user.
Built to show the integration of SAP Business One with Ubudu beacons at SMB Summits 2017.

Based on Ubudu demo app provided as sample:
https://github.com/Ubudu/IOS-SDK
